06Stacking Rules — 75% TGA Ceiling
TGA ceilingTotal Government Assistance cannot exceed 75% of eligible project costs
FedDev capFedDev alone = max 50% of eligible costs per project
OCI + FedDevProvincial + Federal = different government levels, stackable without conflict
IRAP + FedDevIRAP covers salaries / FedDev covers equipment = different cost lines, fully stackable
Scale AI + FedDevSeparate cost buckets required: pilot deployment vs. scale-up costs
Key ruleNever claim the same cost line to two programs
